Admissions

Join our community of knowledge seekers.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (for transfers)
  • Two passport photos
  • Application fee
  • Parent/guardian consent

Procedure

  • Step 1: Obtain form from admin office or online.
  • Step 2: Submit with documents.
  • Step 3: Pay registration fee.
  • Step 4: Attend assessment (if applicable).
  • Step 5: Receive admission letter.

Deadline

Applications for 2025/2026 close 30 July 2025. Apply early!
